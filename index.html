About Me

I am a public-sector consultant with 5+ years of experience advising Deputy Commissioners, Cabinet Ministers, and the Chief Secretariat on translating policy intent into measurable district outcomes across 40+ districts in Punjab and Bihar. I have administered ₹135 Cr+ government portfolios end-to-end — spanning strategy, budgeting, last-mile delivery, monitoring & evaluation, and impact reporting.

My work has engineered marquee turnarounds: elevating Gurdaspur's Aspirational Block from Rank #81 to #1 in zone, and propelling Bihar's iGoT Karmayogi platform from #18 to the #1 national ranking. I hold dual Deputy Commissioner commendations to the Additional Chief Secretary and a Cabinet Minister's commendation — the only fellow in my programme recognised at this seniority.

Currently pursuing the Public Policy Programme at the Indian School of Business (ISB), I am transitioning into senior consulting and advisory roles where governance execution experience meets strategic transformation.
